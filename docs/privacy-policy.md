**Last Updated:** May 25, 2026

Welcome to **Vital30** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use the Vital30 mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Vital30 is a general wellness and habit-tracking application. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide our general wellness and habit challenge features, we collect the following types of information:

### A. Account Data
* When you register an account, we collect personal credentials such as your **name**, **email address**, and **password**.
* This information is used to authenticate your identity, secure your account, and sync your challenge progress across devices.

### B. Challenge Activity
* We collect information about the wellness challenges you join, active habits you target, and your overall challenge progress.
* This includes details about challenge completion dates, durations, and category selections.

### C. Daily Check-ins
* To track your habits, you log check-in statuses (e.g., Completed, Skipped, or Missed) along with optional notes.
* This tracking data is saved to calculate your achievement streaks, progress metrics, and general completion history.

### D. Share Events
* If you utilize the share functionality to celebrate daily progress, invite friends, or post completion statuses, we log the occurrence of these share events (e.g., share type, timestamp).
* *Note:* We do not capture or store the recipient's private contact details or target social accounts, only the event status within Vital30.

### E. Device Information
* We collect technical data from your mobile device, including your device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and language settings.
* This data is used to optimize app performance, troubleshoot bugs, and improve security.

---

## 2. No Sale of Personal Data

We respect your privacy. **We do not sell, rent, trade, or lease your personal data** to third parties for marketing or any other commercial purposes. Your data is strictly utilized to run the Vital30 habit-tracking platform.

## 3. Future AI Features

Please note that we may introduce artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) features in future versions of Vital30 to recommend personalized challenges or analyze wellness habits. 
* Any data processing associated with these future AI/ML tools is **not covered** by this MVP policy.
* We will update this Privacy Policy and request your renewed consent before introducing any AI-driven processing of your personal information.

## 4. Deletion Request Process

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your personal data at any time. 

### How to request deletion:
1. Navigate to your **Profile Screen** in the Vital30 mobile app and tap on **Account Settings** -> **Delete Account**.
2. Alternatively, you can submit a formal deletion request by emailing our support desk at [support@vital30.com](mailto:support@vital30.com) with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
3. Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete or anonymize your account credentials, challenge history, check-in records, and share logs from our active databases within thirty (30) days, subject to any legal holding obligations.
